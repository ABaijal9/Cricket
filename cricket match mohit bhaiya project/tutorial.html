Tutorial: Making a Prediction

This tutorial will guide you through the process of making a prediction for the first innings score in a cricket match using our tool.

  1. **Select the Match Type:** Choose between ODI, T20, or Test match from the dropdown menu. The model uses different data considerations for each format.
  2. **Enter Team Names:** Enter the names of the batting and bowling teams in the respective fields.
  3. **Provide Match Details:** Enter the current score of the batting team and the number of overs bowled (e.g., 13.2 for 13.2 overs).
  4. **Wickets Fallen:** Specify the number of wickets lost by the batting team so far.
  5. **Submit and View Prediction:** Once you've entered all the information, click the "Predict Score" button. Our model will analyze the data and display the predicted score for the first innings.

**Important Notes:**

  • This is a prediction based on historical data and may not be entirely accurate. Actual scores can vary based on various factors during the match.
  • The accuracy of the prediction also depends on the completeness and accuracy of the data entered.
